BRETT KAPLAN'S BIOGRAPHY

Hi, I'm Brett Kaplan, I am proud to be a Syracuse University, class of 2024, Newhouse/iSchool dual major with concentrations in graphic design and information management and technology. These studies reflect my strong interest in the intersection of art and technology, which has evolved into a passion for digital design. It is my goal to one day lead a creative team producing content, digital story-telling, and cultivating ideas for a new generation of media consumers.

PERSONAL INFORMATION

My work experience includes performing as a design assistant for the sports media company, Overtime, where I created web graphics, apparel artwork, and Instagram content for @overtimefits. I am also involved as a graphic design manager for the student-run publication, Fetch Collective; and I create logos, custom wall art, and other material on a freelance basis for a wide range of clientele.
